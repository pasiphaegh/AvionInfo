Parking

Each apartment has allocated parking spaces and these are registered with Bayside Council under a legal arrangement.
Visitor parking is strictly for visitors only and is monitored.

Lifts

The building has multiple lifts serving all levels. For lift emergencies, please use the emergency intercom in the lift or contact building security.

Emergency Procedures

  • Remain calm and press the emergency button
  • Use the intercom to communicate with security
  • Wait for assistance - do not try to force doors open

18 January 2025
Bin fines

Bayside Council introduced a new scanning system when emptying bins into their trucks. They will fine residents when the contents of a bin do not match the bin type. For example, when you throw away soft plastics or put plastic bin liners in the yellow lid recycling bins or shoes in the green lid bins.

  • Yellow lid bins = only individual recyclable items. No soft plastics.
  • Green lid bins = only plants or branchs; no soil, rubbish or recycling.

Please pay attention to avoid a fine. Council fines will be passed to offending tenants and owners.
